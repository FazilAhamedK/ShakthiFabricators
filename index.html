About Us

Established in the year 2008, we, Shakthi Fabricators, are known manufacturers, suppliers and traders of Variac Assembling Sets and Motors, Hylams, SMC Sheets (Sintex make), Heat Sinks and Acrylic Sheets. These products are widely applicable in the industry owing to their premium quality and superior performance. These are commonly used in inverters, UPS, Stabilizer and electrical appliances. Known for their correct performance and smooth operation, these products have earned a high demand in the global market. All our products are tested by a team of quality analyzers to ensure their delay free functioning.
